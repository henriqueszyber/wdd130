Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil

Rio Grande do Sul is a state in southern Brazil known for its strong cultural traditions.

One of the most famous is churrasco, a style of barbecue where different cuts of meat are slowly grilled over open fire.

Another important tradition is chimarrão, a hot herbal drink made from yerba mate and shared socially among friends and family.

Both churrasco and chimarrão represent hospitality, tradition, and community in Rio Grande do Sul.
